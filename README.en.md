# Draw Tarot

English | [日本語](README.md)

A small web app for drawing one tarot card while holding one question in mind.

It shuffles the 22 Major Arcana cards and shows the card at the position you choose.
Each card includes an image reading, positive and negative keywords, and its position on the Tree of Life.

This is less a fortune-telling tool than a way to shift your angle of thought.
Use it to hear another voice inside your own question, not to outsource a conclusion.

## Usage

Open [https://hnsol.github.io/draw-tarot/](https://hnsol.github.io/draw-tarot/).

No account or installation is required. No crystal ball, either.

## Features

- Look at a question from a slightly different angle.
- Turn a vague feeling into words.
- Draw a single card quickly, even during a short break.
- Read from both the card meaning and the card image.
- Use it immediately on mobile or desktop in a browser.
